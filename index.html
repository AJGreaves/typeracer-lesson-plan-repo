TypeRacer Instructions
  1. Select the difficulty level from the dropdown menu.
  2. Read the sample text displayed in the box below the dropdown menu.
  3. Click on the text area and start typing the sample text exactly as shown.
  4. The timer will start automatically when you begin typing.
  5. Press the "Enter" key to end the test and see your results.
  6. Your results will display the time taken and your words per minute (WPM).
  7. Click the "Retry" button to start a new test with a different sample text of the same difficulty level.
